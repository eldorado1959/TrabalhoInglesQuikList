

📱 App Name: QuickList
🎯 Function:
QuickList é um aplicativo simples e direto para criar listas de tarefas diárias. 
Ele foi projetado para pessoas que precisam de uma maneira rápida e eficiente 
de organizar suas atividades sem complicação.

⭐ Main Features:
Task Creation
O usuário pode criar tarefas com títulos, prazos e uma breve descrição. 
A interface permite adicionar, editar e excluir tarefas de forma intuitiva.

Priority Levels
Cada tarefa pode ser marcada com um nível de prioridade: Alta, Média ou Baixa. 
Isso ajuda o usuário a se concentrar no que é mais urgente.

Reminders & Notifications
O aplicativo envia notificações no horário programado para lembrá-lo das tarefas pendentes.

Task Completion
Ao concluir uma tarefa, o usuário pode marcá-la como concluída e ela será movida para uma seção separada, 
com uma lista de todas as tarefas finalizadas.

🖼️ Interface Design (example layout):
(Students should draw or use Canva/Figma for this)

Home Screen:

A list of tasks displayed with their title, priority level, and due date.

A button at the top to Add Task.

Below each task, a checkbox for marking as completed.

Bottom Menu:

Home 🏠 | My Tasks 📋 | Completed ✅ | Settings ⚙️

📱 **App Name:** QuickList  
🎯 **Function:**  

QuickList is a simple and straightforward app for creating daily to-do lists.  
It is designed for people who need a quick and efficient way to organize their tasks without complications.

⭐ **Main Features:**  
**Task Creation**  
Users can create tasks with titles, deadlines, and a brief description. 
The interface allows adding, editing, and deleting tasks intuitively.

**Priority Levels**  
Each task can be assigned a priority level: High, Medium, or Low. 
This helps users focus on the most urgent tasks.

**Reminders & Notifications**  
The app sends notifications at the scheduled time to remind users of pending tasks.

**Task Completion**  
Upon completing a task, users can mark it as done.

**Home Screen:**  
- A list of tasks displayed with their title, priority level, and due date.  
- A button at the top to Add Task.  

**Bottom Menu:**  
- Home 🏠 | My Tasks 📋 | Completed ✅ | Settings ⚙️  


Hi everyone! Our app is called QuickList.  
It helps you quickly create and manage your daily tasks.  
The main features are:  

- **Task Creation**: Easily add tasks with a title, description, and due date.  
- **Priority Levels**: Assign priority to your tasks to focus on what’s most important.  
- **Reminders & Notifications**: Get notified when a task is due.  
- **Task Completion**: Mark tasks as completed, and they’ll move to the "Completed" section.  


🔧 **Usability Focus**  
QuickList is designed to be practical and frictionless, ideal for those who need 
a simple personal organization system without complexity.
